Features

Blockchain based course review system so that your anonymity is never threatened.

Rcoins

Now as an incentive for posting reviews you get Rcoins transferred to you.

Tamper-Resistant

As the platform is based on blockchain technology, the whole system is distributed so it is highly tamper-proof.

Anonymity

Blockchain ensures that your identity is never revealed safeguarding your anonymity.

Course-wise review search powered by Machine Learning

You can now search for reviews of any course you want and see all the reviews of that course.

We also provide a course review summary, using summarization on all the reviews which will help you get an overview of any course.

Along with the reviews we then perform sentiment analysis on the review to determine whether the review is a positive or negative review.
